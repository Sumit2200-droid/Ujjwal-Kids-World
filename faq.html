FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about admissions, curriculum, facilities, and more. Can't find what you're looking for? Feel free to contact us directly.

Ujjwal Kids World accepts students from age 3 to 18, spanning from Pre-Nursery through Class 12. We offer comprehensive education aligned with the CBSE curriculum across all grade levels.

Our early childhood programs (Pre-Nursery to Class 2) focus on foundational learning through play-based activities, while our primary and secondary programs provide rigorous academic preparation.

Our admission process typically begins in December each year for the following academic session. The process includes:

  • Submission of online application form
  • Document verification and review
  • Interaction/assessment (for applicable grades)
  • Admission offer and fee payment
  • Orientation session for new students and parents

We recommend applying early as seats fill quickly. Please contact our admissions office for specific deadlines and requirements.

Ujjwal Kids World follows the CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) curriculum, which is recognized nationwide and provides a strong foundation for higher education in India and abroad.

We complement the CBSE framework with innovative teaching methodologies, project-based learning, and extensive co-curricular activities to ensure holistic development of every student.

Our state-of-the-art campus includes:

  • Smart classrooms with digital learning tools
  • Well-equipped science and computer laboratories
  • Extensive library with digital resources
  • Sports facilities including playground and indoor games
  • Arts and music rooms for creative development
  • Safe and secure campus with CCTV surveillance
  • Comfortable transportation services

We maintain an optimal teacher-to-student ratio to ensure personalized attention for every child. For early years (Pre-Nursery to Class 2), we maintain approximately 1:15 ratio, while for primary and secondary levels, we keep it around 1:25.

This allows our teachers to focus on individual learning needs, identify strengths and areas for improvement, and provide targeted support to help each student reach their full potential.

Yes, we provide safe and reliable transportation services covering Gangarampur and surrounding areas. Our fleet of buses is equipped with GPS tracking, first-aid kits, and trained staff to ensure student safety.

Each bus has an experienced driver and a dedicated attendant. Parents can track bus locations in real-time through our mobile app. Transportation fees are separate from tuition fees.
